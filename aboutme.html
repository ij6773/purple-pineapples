Our mission

Hello! I'm Inchara, a highschool student at Canyon Crest Academy. I've always wanted to be part of the mission to help prevent fake news. So here I am! PurplePineapples is a platform I made for people who wish to write about real news be able to do just that.

Here at PurplePineapples, we strive to inform everyone, whether they be reading or skimming, about current events. Our team consists of highschoolers, all of who share a common goal. Spread real news.

What is our mission?

Though I'm pretty sure I explained this in Our Mission , we strive to spread real, trustworthy news in an easy and readable format. Written by highschoolers and read by you.
