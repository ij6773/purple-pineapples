about us

We are Stardust. A nonprofit dedicated to inspiring students everywhere to pursue careers in astronomy, physics, and much more. We aim to help students of all ages to gain the materials they need to chase their dreams and turn them to reality. At Stardust, we aim to provide free resources for students who have no access to them. 

our goals

At Stardust, we would like to create a friendly environment for students of all ages to come together to solve problems and reach a common goal. Though we focus on topics such as astronomy and physics and anything related, we would like to help students in any way we can. We wish to make resources available for everyone, regardless of location or age. As an organization, our main goal is to help students learn in an accepting environment and support them through their journey and help them pursue their dreams.
